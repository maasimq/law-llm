ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 335
TITLE: Section 335
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Itlaf-i-salahiyyat-i-udw:
Whoever destroys or permanently impairs the functioning,
power or capacity of an organ of the body of another person, or causes permanent
disfigurement is said to cause itlaf-i-salahiyyat-i-udw.
