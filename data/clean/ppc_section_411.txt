ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 411
TITLE: Section 411
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Dishonestly receiving stolen property:
Whoever dishonestly receives or retains,
any stolen property, knowing or having reason to believe the same to be stolen property,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to
three years, or with fine, or with both.
