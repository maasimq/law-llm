ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 185
TITLE: Section 185
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Illegal purchase or bid for property offered for sale by authority of public
servant:
Whoever, at any sale of property held by the lawful authority of a public servant,
as such, purchases or bids for any property on account of any person, whether himself or
"any other, whom he knows to be under a legal incapacity to purchase that property at that
sale, or bids for such property not intending to perform the obligations under which he lays
himself by such bidding, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a
term which may extend to one month, or with fine which may extend to  78[six hundred rupees] 78
rupees, or with both.
