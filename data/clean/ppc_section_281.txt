ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 281
TITLE: Section 281
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Exhibition of false light, mark or buoy:
Whoever exhibits any false light, mark or
buoy intending or knowing it to be likely that such exhibition will mislead any navigator,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to
seven years, or with fine, or with both.
