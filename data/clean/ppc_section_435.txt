ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 435
TITLE: Section 435
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Mischief by fire or explosive substance with intent to cause damage to amount
of one hundred rupees or (in case of agricultural produce) ten rupees:
Whoever
commits mischief by fire or any explosive substance, intending to cause, or knowing it to
be likely that he will thereby cause damage to any property to the amount of one hundred
rupees or upwards  207[or (where the property is agricultural produce) ten rupees or upwards] 207
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which  208[shall not be less
than two years nor more than] 208 seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
