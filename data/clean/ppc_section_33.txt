ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 33
TITLE: "Act", "Omission".
CHAPTER: CHAPTER II — GENERAL EXPLANATIONS
============================================================

"Act", "Omission".
The word "act" denotes as well a series of acts as a single act;
the word "omission" denotes as well a series of omissions as a single omission.
