ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 160
TITLE: Section 160
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for committing affray:
Whoever commits an affray, shall be punished
with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to one month, or with
fine which may extend to  49[three thousand rupees] 49, or with both.
