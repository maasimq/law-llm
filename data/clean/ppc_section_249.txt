ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 249
TITLE: Section 249
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Altering appearance of Pakistan coin with intent that it shall pass as coin of
different description:
Whoever performs on any Pakistan coin any operation which alters
the appearance of that coin, with the intention that the said coin shall pass as a coin of a
different description, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
