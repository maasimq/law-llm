ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 341
TITLE: Section 341
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for wrongful restraint:
Whoever wrongfully restrains any person, shall
be punished with simple imprisonment for a term, which may extend to one month, or with
fine, which may extend to  168[one thousand five hundred rupees] 168 or with both.
