ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 462F
TITLE: Section 462F
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Damaging or destructing the transmission or transportation lines, etc.:

Any person who damages or destructs any transmission or transportation
lines by an act of subversion by explosive material or in any other
manner so as to disrupt the supply of petroleum shall be punished with
rigorous imprisonment which may extend to fourteen years but shall not
be less than seven years and with fine which shall not be less than
one million rupees.
