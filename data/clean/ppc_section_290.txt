ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 290
TITLE: Section 290
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for public nuisance in cases not otherwise provided for:
Whoever
commits a public nuisance in any case not otherwise punishable by this Code, shall be
punished with fine which may extend to  117[six hundred rupees] 117.
