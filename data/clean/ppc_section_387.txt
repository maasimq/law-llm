ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 387
TITLE: Section 387
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Putting person in fear of death or of grievous hurt, in order to commit
extortion:
Whoever, in order to the committing of extortion, puts or attempts to put any
person in fear of death or of grievous hurt to that person or to any Other, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven
years, and shall also be liable to fine.
