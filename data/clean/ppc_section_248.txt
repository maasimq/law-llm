ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 248
TITLE: Section 248
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Altering appearance of coin with intent that it shall pass as coin of different
description:
Whoever performs on any coin any operation which alters the appearance of
that coin, with the intention that the said coin shall pass as a different description, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three
years, and shall also be liable to fine.
