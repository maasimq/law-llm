349.

 Powers with which a Magistrate of the second class may be invested by the Provincial Government.

 (1) Power to record statements and confessions during a police investigation, section 164

 (2) Power to authorise detention of a person in the custody of the police during a police investigation,
section 167.

 (3) power to take cognizance of offences upon complaint, section 190.

 (4) power to take cogniznce of offences upon police report., section 190.

 (5) Power to take congnizance of offences without complaint, section 190.

 Powers wiht which a Magistrate of the third class may be invested by the Provincial Government.

 (1) Power to take cognizance of offences upon compliant, section 190

 (2) Power to take cognizance of offences upon police report, section 190.
