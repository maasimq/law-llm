ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 279
TITLE: Section 279
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Rash driving or riding on a public way:
Whoever drives any vehicle, or rides, on
any public way in a manner so rash or negligent as to endanger human life, or to be likely
to cause hurt or injury to any other person, shall be punished with imprisonment of either
description for a term which may extend to  106[two years] 106 or with fine which may extend to  107[three thousand rupees] 107, or with both.
