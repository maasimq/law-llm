ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 65
TITLE: Section 65
CHAPTER: CHAPTER III — OF PUNISHMENTS.
============================================================

Limit to imprisonment for non-payment of fine when imprisonment and fine
awardable:
The term for which the Court directs the offender to be imprisoned in
default of payment of a fine shall, not exceed one-fourth of the term of imprisonment,
which is the maximum fixed for the offence, if the offence be punishable with
imprisonment as well as fine.
