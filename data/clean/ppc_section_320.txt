ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 320
TITLE: Section 320
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for qatl-i-khata by rash or negligent driving:
Whoever commits qatl-ikhata
by rash or negligent driving shall, having regard to the facts and circumstances the
case, in addition to diyat, be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to ten years.
