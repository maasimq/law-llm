ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 201
TITLE: Section 201
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Causing disappearance of evidence of offence, or giving false information to
screen offender:
Whoever, knowing or having reason to believe that an offence has been
committed, causes any evidence of the commission of that offence to disappear, with the
intention of screening the offender from legal punishment, or with that intention gives any
information respecting the offence which he knows or believes to be false;
