ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 471
TITLE: Section 471
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVIII — OF OFFENCES RELATING TO DOCUMENTS AND TO TRADE OR
            PROPERTY MARKS
============================================================

Using as genuine a forged document:
Whoever fraudulently or dishonestly uses
as genuine any document which he knows or has reason to believe to be a forged
document, shall be punished in the same manner as if he had forged such document.
