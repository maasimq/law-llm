ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 315
TITLE: Section 315
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Qatl shibh-i-amd:
Whoever, with intent to cause harm to the body or mind of any
person, causes the death of that or of any other person by means of a weapon or an act
which in the ordinary course of nature is not likely to cause death is said to commit qatl shibh-i-amd.

Illustration
A in order to cause hurt strikes Z with a stick or stone which in the ordinary course of
nature is not likely to cause death. Z dies as a result of such hurt. A shall be guilty of Qatl
shibh-i-amd.
