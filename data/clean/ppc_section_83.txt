ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 83
TITLE: Section 83
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Act of a child above  29[ten] 29 and under  30[fourteen] 30 of immature understanding:
Nothing
is an offence which is done by a child above  31[ten] 31years of age and under  32[fourteen] 32,
who has not attained sufficient maturity of understanding to judge of the nature and
consequences of his conduct on that occasion.
