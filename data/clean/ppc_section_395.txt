ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 395
TITLE: Section 395
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Punishment for dacoity:
Whoever commits dacoity shall be punished with
imprisonment for life, or with rigorous imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than
four years nor more than ten years and shall also be liable to fine.
