ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 232
TITLE: Section 232
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Counterfeiting Pakistan coin:
Whoever counterfeits, or knowingly performs any
part of the process of counterfeiting Pakistan coin, shall be punished with imprisonment
for life, or with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to ten
years, and shall also be liable to fine.
