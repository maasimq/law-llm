ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 247
TITLE: Section 247
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Fraudulently or dishonestly diminishing weight or altering composition of
Pakistan coin:
Whoever fraudulently or dishonestly performs on any Pakistan coin, any
operation which diminishes the weight or alters the composition of that coin, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven
years, and shall also be liable to fine.
