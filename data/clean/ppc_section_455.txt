ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 455
TITLE: Section 455
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Lurking house-trespass or house-breaking after preparation for hurt, assault or
wrongful restraint:
Whoever commits lurking house-trespass, or house-breaking, having
made preparation for causing hurt to any person, or for assaulting any person, or for
wrongfully restraining any person, or for putting any person in fear of hurt or of assault or
of wrongful restraint, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
