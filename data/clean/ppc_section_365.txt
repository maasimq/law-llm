ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 365
TITLE: Section 365
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Kidnapping or abducting with intent secretly and wrongfully to confine person:
Whoever kidnaps or abducts any person with intent to cause that person to be secretly
and wrongfully confined, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a
term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
