TITLE: Section 392
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Punishment for robbery:
Whoever commits robbery shall be punished with rigorous
imprisonment for a term which  200[shall not be less than three years nor more than] 200 ten years,
and shall also be liable to fine; and, if the robbery be committed on the highway  201[] 201 the
imprisonment may be extended to fourteen years.
