ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 483
TITLE: Section 483
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVIII — OF OFFENCES RELATING TO DOCUMENTS AND TO TRADE OR
            PROPERTY MARKS
============================================================

Counterfeiting a trademark or property mark used by another:
Whoever
counterfeits any trade mark or property mark used by any other person shall be punished
with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years, or with
fine, or with both.
