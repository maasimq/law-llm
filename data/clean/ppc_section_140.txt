ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 140
TITLE: Section 140
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Wearing garb or carrying token used by soldier, sailor or airman:
Whoever, not
being a soldier, sailor or airman in the Military, Navel or Air Service of Pakistan, wear, any
garb or carries any token resembling any garb or token used by such a soldier, sailor or
airman with the intention that it may be believed that he is such a soldier, sailor or airman
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to
three months, or with fine which may extend to  46[one thousand five hundred rupees] 46, or with both.
