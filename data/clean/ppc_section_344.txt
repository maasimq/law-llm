ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 344
TITLE: Section 344
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Wrongful confinement for ten or more days:
Whoever wrongfully confines any
person for ten days or more, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for
a term, which may extend to three years, and shall also be liable to fine.
