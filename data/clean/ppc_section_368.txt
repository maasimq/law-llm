ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 368
TITLE: Section 368
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Wrongfully concealing or keeping in confinement, kidnapped or abducted
person:
Whoever knowing that any person has been kidnapped or has been abducted
wrongfully conceals or confines such person shall be punished in the same manner as if
he had kidnapped or abducted Such person with the same intention or knowledge, or for
the same purposes as that with or for which he conceals or detains such person in
confinement.
