ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 402
TITLE: Section 402
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Assembling for purpose of committing dacoity:
Whoever, at any time after the
passing of this Act shall be one of five or more persons assembled for the purpose of
committing dacoity, shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for a term which may
extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
