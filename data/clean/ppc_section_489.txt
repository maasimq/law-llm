ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 489
TITLE: Section 489
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVIII — OF OFFENCES RELATING TO DOCUMENTS AND TO TRADE OR
            PROPERTY MARKS
============================================================

Tampering with property mark with intent to cause injury:
Whoever removes,
destroys, defaces or adds to any property mark, intending or knowing it to be likely that he
may thereby cause injury to any person, shall be punished with imprisonment of either,
description for a term which may extend to one year, or with fine or with both.
