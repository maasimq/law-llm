ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 336B
TITLE: Section 336B
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for hurt by corrosive substance:

Whoever causes hurt by corrosive substance shall be punished with
imprisonment for life or imprisonment of either description which
shall not be less than fourteen years and a minimum fine of one
million rupees.
