ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 256
TITLE: Section 256
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Having possession of instrument or material for counterfeiting Government
stamp:
Whoever has in his possession any instrument or material for the purpose of
being used, or knowing or having reason to believe that it is intended to be used, for the
purpose of counterfeiting any stamp issued by Government for the purpose of revenue,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to
seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
