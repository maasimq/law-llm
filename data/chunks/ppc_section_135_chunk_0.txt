ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 135
TITLE: Section 135
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Abetment of desertion of soldier, sailor or airman:
Whoever abets the desertion
of any officer, soldier, sailor or airman, in the Army, Navy or Air Force of Pakistan, be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two
years, or with fine, or with both.
