ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 27
TITLE: Property in possession of wife, clerk or servant.
CHAPTER: CHAPTER II — GENERAL EXPLANATIONS
============================================================

Property in possession of wife, clerk or servant.
When property is in the
possession of a person's wife, clerk or servant, on account of that person, it is in that
person's possession within the meaning of this Code.

Explanation: A person employed temporarily on a particular occasion in the capacity of a
clerk, or servant, is a clerk or servant within the meaning of this section.
