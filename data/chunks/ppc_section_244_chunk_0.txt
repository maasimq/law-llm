ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 244
TITLE: Section 244
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Person employed in mint causing coin to be of different weight or composition
from that fixed by law:
Whoever, being employed in any mint lawfully established in
Pakistan, does any act, or omits what he is legally bound to do, with the intention of
causing any coin issued from that mint to be of a different weight or composition from the
weight or composition fixed by law, shall be punished with imprisonment of either
description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
