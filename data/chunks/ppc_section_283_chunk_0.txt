ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 283
TITLE: Section 283
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Danger or obstruction in public way or line of navigation:
Whoever, by doing any
act, or by omitting to take order with any property in his possession or under his charge,
causes danger, obstruction or injury to any person in any public way or public line of
navigation, shall be punished with fine which may extend to  110[six hundred rupees] 110.
