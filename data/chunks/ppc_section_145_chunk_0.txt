ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 145
TITLE: Section 145
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Joining or continuing in unlawful assembly, knowing it has been commanded
to disperse:
Whoever joins or continues in an unlawful assembly, knowing that such
unlawful assembly has been commanded in the manner prescribed by law to disperse,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to
two years, or with fine, or with both.
