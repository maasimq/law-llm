ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 496A
TITLE: Section 496A
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XX — OF OFFENCES RELATING TO MARRIAGE
============================================================

Enticing or taking away or detaining with
criminal intent a woman.
Whoever takes or entices away any woman with
intent that she may have illicit intercourse with any
person, or conceals or detains with that intent any woman,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description
for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also
be liable to fine.
