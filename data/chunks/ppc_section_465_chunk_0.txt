ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 465
TITLE: Section 465
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVIII — OF OFFENCES RELATING TO DOCUMENTS AND TO TRADE OR
            PROPERTY MARKS
============================================================

Punishment for forgery:
Whoever commits forgery shall be punished with
imprisonment of either description for a term, which may extend to two years, or with fine,
or with both.
