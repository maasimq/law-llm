ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 264
TITLE: Section 264
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Fraudulent use of false instrument for weighing:
Whoever fraudulently uses any
instrument for weighing which he knows to be false, shall be punished with imprisonment
of either description for a term, which may extend to one year, or with fine, or with both.
