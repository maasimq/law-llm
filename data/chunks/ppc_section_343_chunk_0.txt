ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 343
TITLE: Section 343
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Wrongful confinement for three or more days:
Whoever wrongfully confines any
person, for three days or more, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description
for a term, which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
