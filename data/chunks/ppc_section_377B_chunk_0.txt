ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 377B
TITLE: Punishment.
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment.
Whoever commits the offence of sexual abuse shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which
197[shall not be less than
fourteen years and may extend up to twenty years and with fine
which shall not be less than one million
rupees.] 197
