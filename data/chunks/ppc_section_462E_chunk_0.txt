ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 462E
TITLE: Section 462E
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Tampering with gas meter by industrial or commerical consumer, etc:

Any person or individual being industrial or commerican consumer who
does tampering or abets in tampering with any gas meter, regulator,
meter index or gas connection or any other related sstem and
equipments, whether to commmit theft of gas or for the purpose of
unauthorized distribution or supply of gas shall be punished with
imprisonment which may extend to ten years but shall not be less than
five years or fine which may extend to five million rupees or both.
