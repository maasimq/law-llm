ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 396
TITLE: Section 396
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Dacoity with murder:
If any one of five or more persons, who are conjointly
committing dacoity, commits murder in so committing dacoity, everyone of those persons
shall be punished with death, or imprisonment for life, or rigorous imprisonment for a term
which  203[shall not be less than four years nor more than] 203 ten years, and shall also be liable
to fine.
