ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 289
TITLE: Section 289
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Negligent conduct with respect to animal:
Whoever, knowingly or negligently omits to take such order
with any animal in his possession as is sufficient to guard against
any probable danger to human life, or any probable danger of grievous
hurt from such animal, shall be punished with imprisonment of either
description for a term which may extend to six months, or with fine
which may extend to  116[three thousand rupees] 116, or with both.
