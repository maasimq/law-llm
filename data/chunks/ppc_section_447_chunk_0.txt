ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 447
TITLE: Section 447
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Punishment for criminal trespass:
Whoever commits criminal trespass shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three
months, or with fine which may extend to  210[one thousand five hundred rupees] 210, or with both.
