ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 76
TITLE: Section 76
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Act done by a person bound, or by mistake of fact believing himself bound, by
law:
Nothing is an offence which Is done by a person who is, or who by reason of a
mistake of fact and not reason of a mistake of law in good faith believes himself to be,
bound by law to do it.
