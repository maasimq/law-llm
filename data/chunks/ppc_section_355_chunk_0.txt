ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 355
TITLE: Section 355
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Assault or criminal force with intent to dishonour person, otherwise than on
grave provocation:
Whoever assaults or uses criminal force to any person, intending
thereby to dishonour that person, otherwise than on grave and sudden provocation given
by that person, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which
may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
