ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 282
TITLE: Section 282
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Conveying person by water for hire in unsafe or overloaded vessel:
Whoever
knowingly or negligently conveys; or causes to be conveyed for hire, any person by water
in any vessel, when that vessel is in such a state or so loaded as to endanger the fife of
that person, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which
may extend to six months, or with fine which may extend to  109[three thousand rupees] 109, or with
both.
