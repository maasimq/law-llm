ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 488
TITLE: Section 488
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVIII — OF OFFENCES RELATING TO DOCUMENTS AND TO TRADE OR
            PROPERTY MARKS
============================================================

Punishment for making use of any such false mark:
Whoever makes use of any
such false mark in any manner prohibited by the last foregoing section shall, unless he
proves that he acted without intent to defraud, be punished as if he had committed an
offence against that section.
