ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 506
TITLE: Section 506
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XXII — OF CRIMINAL INTIMIDATION, INSULT AND ANNOYANCE
============================================================

Punishment for criminal intimidation:
Whoever commences the offence of criminal
intimidation shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which
may extend to two years or with fine or with both.
