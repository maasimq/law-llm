ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 438
TITLE: Section 438
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Punishment for the mischief described in Section 437 committed by fire or
explosive substance:
Whoever commits, or attempts to commit, by fire or any explosive
substance, such mischief as is described in the last preceding section, shall be punished
with imprisonment for life or with imprisonment of either description for a term which may
extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
