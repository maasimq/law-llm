ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 127
TITLE: Section 127
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Receiving property taken by war or depredation mentioned in Sections 125
and 126:
Whoever receives any property knowing the same to have been taken in the
commission of any of the offences mentioned in Sections 125 and 126, shall be punished
with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and
shall also be liable to fine and forfeiture of the property so received.
