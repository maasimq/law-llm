ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 325
TITLE: Section 325
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XXIII — OF ATTEMPTS TO COMMIT OFFENCES
============================================================

Attempt to commit suicide:
Whoever attempts to commit suicide and does any act
towards the commission of such offence, shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a
term which may extend to one year, or with fine, or with both.
