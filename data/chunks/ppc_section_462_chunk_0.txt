ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 462
TITLE: Section 462
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Punishment for same offence when committed by person entrusted with
custody:
Whoever, being entrusted with any dosed receptacle which contains or which he
believes to contain property, without having authority to open the same, dishonestly, or
with intent to commit mischief, breaks open or unfastens that receptacle, shall be
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three years,
or with fine, or with both.
