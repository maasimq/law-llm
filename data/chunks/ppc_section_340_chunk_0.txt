ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 340
TITLE: Section 340
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Wrongful confinement:
Whoever wrongfully restrains any person in such a manner
as 10 prevent that person from proceeding beyond certain circumscribing limits, is said
"wrongfully to confine" that person.
