ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 469
TITLE: Section 469
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVIII — OF OFFENCES RELATING TO DOCUMENTS AND TO TRADE OR
            PROPERTY MARKS
============================================================

Forgery for purpose of harming reputation:
Whoever commits forgery, intending
that the document forged shall harm the reputation of any party, or knowing that it is likely
to be used for that purpose, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a
term which may extend to three years, and shall also be liable to fine.
