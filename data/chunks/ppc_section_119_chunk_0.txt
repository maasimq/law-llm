ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 119
TITLE: Section 119
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Public servant concealing design to commit offence which it is his duty to
prevent:
Whoever, being a public servant intending to facilitate or knowing it to be likely
that he will thereby facilitate the commission of an offence which it is his duty as such
public servant to prevent, voluntarily conceals, by any act or illegal omission, the
existence of a design to commit such offence, or makes any representation which he
knows to be false respecting such design,
