ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 342
TITLE: Section 342
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for wrongful confinement:
Whoever wrongfully confines any person,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for, a term, which may extend to
one year, or with fine which may extend to  169[three thousand rupees] 169 or with both.
