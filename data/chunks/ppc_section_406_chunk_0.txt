ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 406
TITLE: Section 406
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Punishment for criminal breach of trust:
Whoever, commits criminal breach of
trust snail be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may
extend to  205[seven] 205 years, or with fine, or with both.
