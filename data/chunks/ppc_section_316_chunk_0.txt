ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 316
TITLE: Section 316
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for Qatl shibh-i-amd:
Whoever commits qatl shibh-i-amd shall be
liable to diyat and may also be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to  154[twenty-five years] 154 years as ta'zir.
