ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 385
TITLE: Section 385
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Putting person in fear of injury in order to commit extortion:
Whoever, in order
to the committing of extortion, puts any, person in fear, or attempts to put any person in
fear, of any injury, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
