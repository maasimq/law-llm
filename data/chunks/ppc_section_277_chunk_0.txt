ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 277
TITLE: Section 277
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Fouling water of public spring or reservoir:
Whoever voluntarily corrupts or fouls
the water of any public spring or reservoir, so as to render it less fit for the purpose for
which it is ordinarily used, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a
term which may extend to three months, or with fine which may extend to  104[one thousand five hundred rupees] 104, or with both.
