ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 428
TITLE: Section 428
CHAPTER: CHAPTER XVII — OF OFFENCES AGAINST PROPERTY
============================================================

Mischief by killing or maiming animal of the value of ten rupees:
Whoever
commits mischief by killing, poisoning, maiming or rendering useless any animal of the
value of ten rupees or upwards, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description
for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
