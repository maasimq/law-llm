ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 369
TITLE: Section 369
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Kidnapping or abducting child under ten years with intent to steal from its
person:
Whoever kidnaps or abducts any child under the age of ten years with the
intention of taking dishonestly any movable property from the person of such child, shall
be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven
years, and shall also be liable to fine.
