ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 310A
TITLE: Section 310A
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Punishment for giving a female in marriage or otherwise in badal-i-sulh, wanni or swara:
Whoever gives a female in marriage or otherwise compels her to enter into marriage, as
badal-i-sulh, wanni or swara or any other custom or practice under any name, in consideration of settling a civil dispute or a criminal liability, shall
be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years but shall not be less than
three years and shall also be liable to fine of five hundred thousand rupees..
