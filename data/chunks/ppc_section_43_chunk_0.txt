ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 43
TITLE: Section 43
CHAPTER: CHAPTER II — GENERAL EXPLANATIONS
============================================================

"Illegal", "Legally bound to do":
The word "illegal" is applicable to everything which is
an offence or which is prohibited by law, or which furnishes ground for a civil action, and a
person is said to be "legally bound to do" whatever it is illegal in him to omit.
