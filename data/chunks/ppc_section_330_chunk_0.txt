ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 330
TITLE: Section 330
CHAPTER: CHAPTER IV — GENERAL EXCEPTIONS
============================================================

Disbursement of diyat:
The diyat shall be disbursed among the heirs of the victim
according to their respective shares in inheritance:
Provided that, where an heir foregoes his share, the diyat shall not be recovered to the
extent of his share.
