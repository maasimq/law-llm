ACT: Pakistan Penal Code, 1860
SECTION/ARTICLE: 5
TITLE: Certain laws not to be affected by this Act.
CHAPTER: CHAPTER I — INTRODUCTION
============================================================

Certain laws not to be affected by this Act.
Nothing in this Act is intended to repeal, vary, suspend or
affect any of the provisions of any Act for punishing mutiny and
desertion of officers, soldiers, sailors or airmen in the service of
the State or of any special or local law.
